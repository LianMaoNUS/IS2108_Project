Our Story

AuroraMart was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to make quality shopping accessible to everyone in Southeast Asia. Backed by venture capital and powered by cutting-edge AI technology, we're committed to delivering personalized shopping experiences that keep customers coming back.

Our name "Aurora" represents the dawn of a new era in e-commerce - one that's bright, personalized, and accessible to all.
